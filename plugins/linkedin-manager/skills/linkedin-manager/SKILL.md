# LinkedIn Manager

Build authority and grow your professional network with high-performing LinkedIn content.

---

## Authentication

LinkedIn management uses the LinkedIn API.
- Check if connected via Settings → Connectors → LinkedIn
- If not: "Connect LinkedIn in Settings → Connectors for direct posting. Until then, I can write all your posts and copy them for you to paste manually."

---

## The LinkedIn Algorithm — Key Facts

1. **First hour engagement is critical** — comments from your network in the first 60 minutes determine reach
2. **Comments > likes > reactions** — long comments especially boost distribution
3. **External links get punished** — posting links (to articles, websites) massively reduces reach. Put links in comments.
4. **Native content wins** — LinkedIn prefers documents, carousels, native video over external links
5. **Consistency beats frequency** — 3x/week consistently beats 7x/week then nothing
6. **Niche authority builds over time** — consistent topic area = LinkedIn recommends you to more people in that space

## Post Types & What Each Does

| Format | Strength | Use for |
|--------|----------|---------|
| **Text post** | Quick, authentic | Hot takes, observations, short stories |
| **Document/Carousel** | High saves, high reach | Frameworks, lessons, step-by-steps |
| **Native video** | Highest reach potential | Behind-the-scenes, talking head, tutorials |
| **Poll** | High engagement | Quick opinions, market research |
| **Article** | SEO + long-form authority | Deep dives, evergreen content |
| **Newsletter** | Subscriber relationship | Regular updates, deep expertise |

## Post Writing Guide

### Structure for Text Posts
```
[HOOK — first 2 lines, visible before "see more"]

[Body — story, lesson, or argument]

[List or white space for readability]
• Point 1
• Point 2
• Point 3

[Closing insight or reflection]

[CTA — subtle: "What do you think?" / "Drop your experience below"]
```

### Hook Formulas for LinkedIn
- **Controversial**: "Hot take: [contrarian opinion in your field]"
- **Story opener**: "3 years ago, I [made mistake / learned lesson / took risk]."
- **Curiosity**: "Most people in [industry] don't know this."
- **List tease**: "5 lessons from [X years] in [field]:"
- **Confession**: "I used to think [wrong belief]. Here's what changed my mind."
- **Question**: "Why do [smart/successful] people still [do ineffective thing]?"

### Tone for LinkedIn
- **Conversational but professional** — write like you talk, not like a press release
- **First person** — "I" not "we" performs better
- **No corporate jargon** — "synergize", "leverage", "paradigm shift" kill engagement
- **Vulnerability works** — sharing failures and lessons gets more engagement than wins alone
- **No hashtag spam** — 2–3 relevant hashtags max, at the end

## Profile Optimization

### Headline Formula
```
[What you do] | [Who you help] | [Unique angle or result]
```
Example: "Helping B2B founders close more deals through content | 3x your pipeline without paid ads"

### About Section Structure
1. Hook (who you are + bold claim)
2. Who you help + how
3. Proof / credibility (results, experience, numbers)
4. What you offer / current focus
5. CTA (DM me, visit website, etc.)

### Featured Section
- Pin your best post, a lead magnet, or your most important link
- Keep it updated — stale pins hurt credibility

## Outreach & Connection Messages

**Connection request note (under 300 chars):**
```
Hi [Name], I came across your post about [X] and it really resonated.
I'm also working in [related space] — would love to connect.
```

**Follow-up DM after connecting:**
```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]!
I've been following your work on [X] — [genuine specific observation].
[Optional: offer value or relevant resource without asking for anything]
```

**Rule**: Never pitch in the first message. Add value first.

## Analytics

Key LinkedIn metrics:
- **Impressions** — How many times shown
- **Reactions + Comments + Shares** — Engagement quality indicators
- **Post click-through** — How many visited your profile after the post
- **Follower growth from content** — Which posts drive follows

## Common Workflows

### "Write me a LinkedIn post"
1. Ask: topic, audience, goal (thought leadership, engagement, leads)
2. Write post with hook, body, CTA
3. Offer 2 versions: short punchy vs longer story-driven
4. Remind: no external links in post — put in first comment

### "Optimize my LinkedIn profile"
1. Ask for current headline, about, and target audience
2. Rewrite headline with formula
3. Draft new About section
4. Suggest featured section content

### "Grow my LinkedIn following"
1. Ask: current follower count, niche, posting frequency
2. Give 30-day content plan
3. Identify content pillars (3–4 themes to post about consistently)
4. Engagement strategy: who to comment on, how to use polls
