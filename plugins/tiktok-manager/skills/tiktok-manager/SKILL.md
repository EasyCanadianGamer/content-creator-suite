## The TikTok Algorithm — Key Facts

Understanding this drives every recommendation:

1. **Completion rate is king** — TikTok shows your video to more people if they watch it to the end
2. **Shares > Likes** — Shares signal the content is worth spreading
3. **Re-watches matter** — Videos that get rewatched rank higher
4. **First 3 seconds decide everything** — If the hook doesn't hold, people scroll
5. **Niche consistency** — Consistent content type trains the algorithm who to show you to
6. **Posting time matters less than engagement velocity** — First hour of engagement is the key signal

## Hook Writing

The hook is the #1 determinant of performance. Always write 3 hook options:

### Hook Formulas
- **Bold claim**: "This changed everything about how I [X]"
- **Controversy**: "You've been doing [X] wrong your whole life"
- **Curiosity gap**: "Nobody talks about what happens when you [X]"
- **POV**: "POV: You finally figured out [X]"
- **Number**: "3 things I wish someone told me about [X]"
- **Question**: "Why does [common thing] actually [surprising result]?"
- **Storytime**: "The day I [dramatic event] and what it taught me"
- **Before/after**: "I did [X] for 30 days. Here's what changed."

### On-Screen Text Hook (first frame)
- Should be different from the spoken hook but complementary
- Bold, large text, high contrast
- Under 8 words
- Creates a second reason to stop scrolling

## Caption Writing

TikTok captions are short — 150 characters sweet spot.

**Formula**: [Hook or context] + [soft CTA] + [2–5 hashtags]

Example:
```
I can't believe I waited this long to try this 😭 save this before you need it

#productivity #lifehack #worktips #fyp
```

**Hashtag strategy for TikTok:**
- 3–5 hashtags max (unlike Instagram)
- 1–2 niche tags, 1–2 topic tags, always #fyp or #foryou
- Don't overload — TikTok's algorithm reads captions, not just hashtags

## Analytics Interpretation

Key TikTok metrics:
- **Average watch time** — aim for 70%+ of video length
- **Completion rate** — % who watch to end (most important)
- **Shares** — strongest growth signal
- **Traffic source** — FYP vs profile vs search vs follows
- **Follower activity** — when your audience is online

### Diagnosing underperformance:
- Stuck at ~200–300 views? → First batch of viewers didn't engage enough → Repost with better hook
- High views, no follows? → Content isn't niche-clear → More consistent topic/style needed
- Good views, low completion? → Content is too long or loses steam → Tighten the middle
