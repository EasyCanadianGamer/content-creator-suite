# Content Ideas Generator

Produces platform-specific, audience-targeted content ideas with hooks, angles, formats, and optional full content calendars.

---

## Step 1: Gather Context

Before generating, confirm these inputs. If missing, ask the user:

| Input | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| **Niche / topic area** | What the content is about (fitness, finance, cooking, gaming, etc.) |
| **Platform(s)** | YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, or all |
| **Audience** | Who they're targeting (age, interests, pain points) |
| **Goal** | Growth, engagement, sales, brand awareness, community |
| **Volume** | How many ideas? One-off or full calendar? |

If the user gives a vague brief, generate 10 ideas across formats and ask which direction resonates.

---

## Step 2: Research Angles

For each niche, generate ideas across these proven angles:

### The 7 Core Content Angles
1. **Educational** — "How to X", "X explained", "The truth about X"
2. **Controversial / Hot take** — "Why X is wrong", "Unpopular opinion: X"
3. **Listicle** — "5 ways to X", "Top 10 X"
4. **Story / Personal** — "How I X", "My journey with X", "What happened when I X"
5. **Trend-jack** — Tie your niche to a trending topic or format
6. **Behind-the-scenes** — Process, day-in-the-life, making-of
7. **Community / Engagement** — Polls, questions, challenges, duets/collabs

---

## Step 3: Platform-Specific Formats

Tailor ideas to what performs on each platform:

### YouTube
- Long-form (8–20 min): Deep dives, tutorials, essays, vlogs
- Shorts (under 60s): Quick tips, hooks, teasers
- Series potential: Multi-part content for subscribers to follow

### TikTok
- Trends first: hook must hit in 1–2 seconds
- Formats: POV, storytime, "things I wish I knew", duet/stitch bait
- Audio: suggest trending sounds where relevant

### Instagram
- Reels: Short punchy video (same as TikTok logic)
- Carousels: Educational slides, lists, step-by-steps
- Stories: Behind-the-scenes, polls, Q&A
- Feed posts: Aesthetic, quote graphics, single-image value

### LinkedIn
- Thought leadership, personal story, industry insight
- Hook-first text posts (no hashtag spam)
- Carousels for frameworks and lessons

### Twitter/X
- Threads: Step-by-step, lists, storytelling
- Single tweets: Hot takes, questions, observations
- Quote tweets: Commentary on trending topics

---

## Step 4: Output Format

### For idea lists, output like this:

```
💡 CONTENT IDEAS: [Niche] — [Platform]

1. [Title / Hook]
   Format: [Reel / Long-form / Thread / etc.]
   Angle: [Educational / Controversial / etc.]
   Hook: "[Opening line or first 3 seconds]"
   Why it works: [1 sentence]

2. ...
```

### For full content calendars, output a weekly or monthly table:

| Day | Platform | Format | Title / Topic | Angle | Hook |
|-----|----------|--------|---------------|-------|------|
| Mon | YouTube  | Long-form | ... | ... | ... |
| Tue | TikTok   | Short | ... | ... | ... |

Then offer to generate scripts or captions for any idea.

---

## Step 5: Extras to Offer

After delivering ideas, offer:
- "Want me to write a full script for any of these?"
- "Want me to generate a thumbnail concept and title for any?"
- "Want me to turn this into a full month's content calendar?"
- "Want me to research what's currently trending in [niche]?" (use web search)

---

## Tips

- **Stuck niche**: If the user doesn't have a clear niche, help them identify one based on their skills, interests, and audience.
- **Repurposing**: Always suggest how one idea can be repurposed across platforms (YouTube → Shorts → LinkedIn → Twitter thread).
- **Trending topics**: Use web search to find what's currently trending in the niche before generating.
- **Competitor analysis**: If the user mentions competitors, analyze what's working for them.
